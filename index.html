About me

Hello. My name is Lukas Curtis. I am an aspiring artist currently enrolled at the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire. The rest of this box will be filled with placeholder text since the real Lukas Curtis hasn't gotten around to providing the real content that will eventually be placed here.

I mean, I don't know his life story. But I can try to write down everything I know about him. I have a beard. I normally am wearing glasses. Uhh what else... I currently work at Noodles n Company, though I sort of hate it. But I did meet a lot of awesome people there including Ryan Vaughan (oh yeah and my girlfriend).

As for my art, I have a particular fondess for character sketches and hope to work as part of the storyboarding process for either videogame design or animated show design. I'm pretty dang good at it too so if you're reading this you should hire me before it's too late.

Be careful to believe anything I say because most of what comes out of my mouth is just bull!@#$ and part of some prank or long-con. I have a very unsettling slightly smaller than life-size mannequin of Santa Claus that I like to leave around the corner of unlit rooms in my apartment, and somehow you always forget about it and nearly have a heart attack when you enter the room.

Yeah I think those four paragraphs just about sum up every single detail about my life. So that's all folks. Be sure to subscribe and smash that like button (but no really check out my art and feel free to contact me via the links or the email below!)
